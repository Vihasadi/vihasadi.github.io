Semantic Segmentation of Brain Tumors

Deep learning-based semantic segmentation of brain tumors in MRI scans using U-Net architectures.

Tech Stack

  • Python, PyTorch / TensorFlow
  • U-Net architecture
  • Data augmentation and advanced loss functions

Approach

  • Preprocess MRI scans (normalization, resizing).
  • Train U-Net on labeled tumor regions.
  • Use Dice loss / IoU-based metrics to evaluate segmentation performance.
