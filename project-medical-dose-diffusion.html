Medical Dose Diffusion Model

A diffusion-based deep learning model to predict radiotherapy dose distribution for Head & Neck cancer patients, inspired by DoseDiff and extended with MambaVision components.

Tech Stack

  • PyTorch, DoseDiff, MambaVision
  • NumPy, medical image preprocessing
  • Evaluation: SSIM, PSNR, DVH metrics

Problem

Radiotherapy planning requires accurate dose distribution to maximize tumor control while protecting organs-at-risk. Manual planning is time-consuming, and deep learning models can accelerate decision-making.

Approach

  • Use CT and structure maps as inputs.
  • Train a diffusion model to generate realistic dose maps.
  • Evaluate predicted dose against ground truth using dose and DVH metrics.
