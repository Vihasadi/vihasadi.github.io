AI Research Paper Summarizer

A Generative AI system that retrieves research papers, generates structured summaries, and provides automated review insights using LLMs and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

Tech Stack

  • Python, LangChain, OpenAI / LLM APIs
  • Vector Databases: FAISS / Chroma
  • Embeddings, Prompt Engineering

What It Does

  • Accepts a research topic or paper link as input.
  • Retrieves relevant research papers using search or local corpus.
  • Chunks and embeds sections for semantic search.
  • Generates section-wise summaries and a structured “review-style” output.

Highlights

  • RAG architecture with context-aware answers.
  • Designed for students/researchers to quickly understand long papers.
  • Can be extended into a full academic assistant or literature review tool.
